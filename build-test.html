PLANET SURFACE FLOOR LAB PICK A TONE

Four planet-surface candidates for the live game. All share the grounded-shadow logic (Sphere FLOATS, Tower + Hulk GROUNDED) and side-themed shadow tints (defender blue, cyborg red). Space- station floors will be a future test row.

1. COOL TACTICAL

Dark blue-gray gradient. Modern XCOM tactical-board feel. Pieces pop because nothing competes with them tonally.
#1F2733 to #161C26

2. DUSTY PLANET

Lighter warm sand-tan. Closest to the current Perlin dirt without the noise. Reads as a Mars-style desert planet.
#4A3E2E to #322820

3. DARK PLANET

Deep-space night-side surface. Near-black with a faint blue glow. Maximum piece contrast — pieces feel lit up against the surface.
#0A0E16 to #050810

4. ZONE-TINTED

Cool planet base with subtle blue bleed on the defender (left) side and red bleed on the cyborg (right) side. Side identity baked into the floor.
BLUE LEFT / RED RIGHT
