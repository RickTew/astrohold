MINI CONTROL CENTER FINAL DESIGN

Speed dial on the outer arc (click any of the 3 tick dots — amber = slow, cyan = normal, red = fast). 4 toggles inside (Music / SFX / Speech bubbles / Combat log). BATTLE pill at the bottom is the primary action — clicking it toggles to PAUSE. In production this will be context-aware (BATTLE during BUILD, PAUSE during reveal, PLAY AGAIN after game end).

CALLOUT MATRIX — UNIT VOICE LINES DESIGN REVIEW

Every situational callout in the game, grouped by trigger. Each cell shows the 3-4 random options a unit can say. {n} substitutes the current count; {s}/{S} auto-pluralizes ("" if n=1, else "s"/"S"). Click any line to pop a sample bubble on the canvas so you can read it at battle scale.
