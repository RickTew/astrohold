AstroHold — Battle Stats

Reads localStorage["astrohold:battle-stats:v1"], populated by BattleStats.recordBattle at the end of every game. Play 10–20 battles, then come back here to spot trends.
The COPY JSON button puts everything on your clipboard so you can paste it into chat for analysis. CLEAR wipes the record set (useful when starting a new balance test).

SUMMARY

BALANCE HEALTH

One row per piece-type per side, aggregated across all recorded matches. The status flag points to outliers: 🔥 OVERPERFORMER (dmg/cr above 2x its side average), ❄ UNDERPERFORMER (below 0.5x), ⚠ AMMO OVERFLOW (avg shots above 1.5x base ammo, ie crate pickups carrying it). Win rate is the side's win rate in matches where this piece was deployed; compare against the side baseline at top of the row group.

ASSISTS BY PIECE TYPE

Damage delivered to a target someone else killed. High assist + low kill = setting up other pieces.

ATTACKS / MOVEMENT BY PIECE TYPE

Engagement signal. Attacks = total fire/throw/slam actions. Cells walked = move steps. Low movement + low attacks = piece sat idle.

COST EFFECTIVENESS

Damage per credit spent across all games + kills per 100cr. Ranks how much value each piece type delivers per cost.

ENEMY-ELIMINATED TURN

Turn at which the enemy side reached 0 alive units. Compare to total turns — gap means the winning side took extra turns after eliminating the enemy (e.g. attacker cleared defenders but still needed turns to reach the core).

ACTION COUNTS

ERROR-HUNTING TELEMETRY

Aggregated across all recorded games. Look for unexpected entries. Per-side resupply mismatches mean an AI advantage. Friendly fire counts mean AoE targeting bugs. One-shot victims that are NOT Sniper kills mean a damage bug. Damage reconciliation gaps mean a code path bypassing attribute().

PER-PIECE BY SIDE (S17.14)

Same data as ERROR-HUNTING TELEMETRY but split by side, so `cannon` on the cyborg side does not collide with `cannon` on the defender side. Only populated for games recorded after the S17.14 telemetry expansion.

PER-PIECE DETAIL

Pick a piece type to see every metric for it across all games. Catches per-piece outliers the headline summary misses.

GRENADIER + BOMBER THROW LANDINGS

Every recorded throw. distFromEnemy is the world-units distance from the landing cell to the nearest enemy at throw time. Low values mean throws are landing near enemies (good). High values mean throws are flying off into dead air (broken targeting).

HULK CORE PROGRESS

Per Hulk: starting X (east edge) and ending X. Negative delta means the Hulk moved west toward the defender core (intended). Near-zero delta means the Hulk got stuck or never moved.
